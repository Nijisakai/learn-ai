KOFAC have produced ten textbooks for the Korean Software Education Module which cover some interesting, cutting edge topics with integrated ‘unplugged’ and programming exercises.

Here are the topics:

1 Artificial Intelligence

2 Driverless vehicles

3 Internet of things

4 Virtual reality

5 CRISPR

6 Space launch vehicles

7 Natural disasters

8 Smart medicine

9 Game engines

10 Sports statistics

Some reference Entry, which is a Korean equivalent of Scratch: it can be used in English, Korean and Vietnamese.

I’ve uploaded the PDFs here with permissions; they’re free for educational use only. Note that copyright is held by the Korean Ministry of Science, ICT and Future Planning, Korea Foundation for the Advancement of Science and Creativity
